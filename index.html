Questions

Welcome to Doppelganger.


Doppelganger asks a series of questions, and based on the answers to those questions, it will create an image that is just like you. Once you create the image, you can click the Save button in the lower left to have your image open in a new window. Please right-click to save it to your computer.


Also, feel free to email Doppelganger to a friend using the button to the right.



Doppelganger creates its life like replicas with help of:


  • HTML5 & CSS3 - Soon to be the new standards for the web.
  • doodle.js - A sprite based javascript library for drawing in the canvas.
  • SimpleModal - A jQuery modal box implementation.
